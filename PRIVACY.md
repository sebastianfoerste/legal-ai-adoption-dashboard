# Privacy

This app processes no personal data and no client data.

- Every account, practice group, blocker, and feedback item in `data/` is synthetic and
  invented for demonstration. No real firm, in-house team, or individual is represented.
- Personas are aggregate seat counts, not people.
- Nothing is collected, stored, or transmitted at runtime. The app renders bundled
  synthetic JSON.

The dashboard is designed around the principle that legal AI sits behind a human-review
gate: it tracks adoption of a reviewed product and never stands in for legal advice.
